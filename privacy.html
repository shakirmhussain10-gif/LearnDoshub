Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 27, 2026

At LearnDocs Hub, your privacy is our top priority. This policy explains how our website handles your data (short answer: we don't collect it).

1. No Data Collection

LearnDocs Hub does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. We do not require account creation, email addresses, or any form of registration. You can use all 100+ tools without providing any personal information.

2. Browser-Based Processing

All file processing happens entirely in your web browser using client-side JavaScript. This means:

  • Your files are never uploaded to our servers or any third-party server.
  • All conversions, edits, and transformations happen on your device.
  • When you close the browser tab, all processed data is gone.
  • We have zero access to your files, documents, or images.

3. No Cookies for Tracking

We do not use tracking cookies, analytics cookies, or advertising cookies. The only data stored locally is your theme preference (saved in your browser's localStorage), which is never transmitted anywhere.

4. Anonymous Analytics

We use Firebase Analytics (Google) to understand how users interact with our website. This data is:

  • Anonymous - we cannot identify individual users
  • Aggregated - only collective statistics are collected
  • Non-personal - no names, emails, or file data

This helps us improve our tools. You can opt out of analytics by disabling JavaScript in your browser.

5. AI Chat Assistant

Our AI Chat Assistant helps you with questions, writing, coding, and finding the right tools. When you use the chat:

  • Your messages are processed to provide responses
  • We do not store your chat history
  • Do not share sensitive personal information in chat

6. External Libraries

Our tools use open-source JavaScript libraries loaded from CDN providers (such as cdnjs.cloudflare.com) to provide functionality like PDF processing and QR code generation. These CDN requests are standard web requests and do not transmit your file data.

6. Google Fonts

We use Google Fonts to display text on our website. When you visit our site, your browser makes a request to Google's servers to load the font files. This is a standard web practice. Google's privacy policy applies to this request.

7. Advertising

LearnDocs Hub displays advertisements to support our free services. Third-party ad networks may use cookies to display relevant ads. You can manage ad preferences through your browser settings.

8. Children's Privacy

Since we do not collect any data, there are no special considerations for children's privacy. Our tools are safe for users of all ages.

9. Data Security

Because all processing happens in your browser and no data is transmitted to our servers, the security of your files depends on your own device's security. We recommend keeping your browser and operating system updated.

10. Changes to This Policy

If we make any changes to this privacy policy, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. Since we don't collect email addresses, we encourage you to review this page periodically.

11. Contact

If you have any questions about this privacy policy or our practices, you can reach out to the creator, Muhammad Hussain Shakir, through the About page.
