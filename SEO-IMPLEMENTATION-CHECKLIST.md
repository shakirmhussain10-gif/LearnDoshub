## 🚨 Critical Don'ts

- ❌ Don't change page URLs without 301 redirects
- ❌ Don't duplicate content across pages
- ❌ Don't keyword stuff (keep keywords natural)
- ❌ Don't hide text or use cloaking
- ❌ Don't use redirect chains
- ❌ Don't create multiple sitemaps without purpose
- ❌ Don't change title/description randomly
- ❌ Don't forget to update sitemap when adding pages
- ❌ Don't submit form data to search engines
- ❌ Don't disable image indexing
