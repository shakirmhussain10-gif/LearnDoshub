## 🎓 Key Concepts

### Breadcrumbs
Google sees: Home > Tools > PDF Tools > PDF Merger
Helps users understand site structure

### Schema Markup
Tells Google: This is a software application, it's free, it has a 4.8 rating
Enables rich search results

### Sitemaps
Maps of all your pages
Submitted to Google Search Console for priority crawling

### Meta Tags
Title, description, keywords
What appears in Google search results

### Internal Linking
Links between your pages
Improves crawlability
